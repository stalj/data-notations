Krakow University of Economics

History

Founded in 1925, Krakow University of Economics is one of the most prestigious economic universities in Poland. It has a rich history of educating experts in economics and finance.

Study Programs

The university offers a wide range of programs, including finance, economics, business analytics, and international relations, tailored to meet the demands of the modern job market.

Infrastructure

With modern facilities like the Main Library and conference centers, KUE provides excellent conditions for both study and research in the heart of Cracow.

Research

KUE is known for its research in economics and management sciences. The university actively collaborates with international partners and conducts groundbreaking studies.

Student Life

KUE offers a vibrant student life with numerous student organizations, sports clubs, and cultural events. The university is located near key areas of Cracow, enhancing the student experience.
